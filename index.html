How the Migration Works

Four structured steps to move to Zoho Mail without downtime or data loss. Your website is not affected.

01
DNS

Update DNS Records

Change MX, SPF, and DKIM records in your domain registrar or cPanel Zone Editor to point to Zoho's mail servers.

02
IMAP

Migrate Email History

Use Zoho's built-in IMAP migration tool to import existing emails from cPanel before the old plan is cancelled.

03
Users

Onboard Your Team

Each person downloads the Zoho Mail app or reconfigures Outlook / Thunderbird using new credentials from the admin.

04
Hosting

Keep Your Website

Your website stays on cPanel. Moving email away may allow you to downgrade to a cheaper hosting plan as well.
